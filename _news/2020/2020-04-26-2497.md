Diagram of the scientific research, development and training enterprise

SAGE2 is a widely known open-source environment for collaborative immersive analytics on large display walls. This paper briefly outlines our group&rsquo;s approach to evolving the SAGE2 software to support existing and future collaborative scientific exploration workflows through the application of Artificial Intelligence in Immersive Analytics.<br><br>
<strong>Author Keywords:</strong>  Immersive Analytics, Large Display Walls, SAGE
